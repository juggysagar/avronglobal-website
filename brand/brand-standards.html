Executive Summary

Avron Global requires a brand identity system that signals sovereign-level credibility without claiming regulated status. The system must function across three imperatives: regulatory safety, institutional authority, and GCC-London credibility.

Strategic Imperatives

Imperative Requirement Execution
Regulatory Safety No implied investment advisory or brokerage Language audited; disclaimers prominent
Institutional Authority Comparable to KKR, Carlyle, Blackstone Navy dominance, classical typography, restraint
GCC-London Credibility Heritage and global reach simultaneously Brass accent, Bahrain reference, City of London registration

Competitive Position

We occupy the space between management consultancy (McKinsey, Bain) and investment banking (Lazard, Evercore)—closer to the former in structure, closer to the latter in outcome.

Brand Promise

"We serve few, deeply." Selective engagement. Multi-year partnerships. Senior-partner attention.

Color Psychology

  • Navy: Trust, permanence, institutional memory. Used by KKR, McKinsey.
  • Brass: Warmth, heritage, discretion. Distinctive—competitors use gold (ostentatious).
  • Sand: Elegance, restraint, readability. Luxury hospitality parallel (Aman, Four Seasons).

Regulatory Compliance & Implementation

Prohibited Language

Never Use Replacement
"Investment advisor" "Strategic advisor"
"Wealth management" "Strategic growth advisory"
"Capital raising" (alone) "Advisory on capital formation"
"Success fee" (alone) "Engagement fee structure"
"Proprietary deals" "Sourced opportunities"

Required Qualifying Language

First Contact

"We do not manage capital. We do not execute transactions. We advise on the architecture of growth."

Implementation Roadmap

Phase Timeline Key Deliverables
Foundation Weeks 1-2 Website, email signatures, Word template
Collateral Weeks 3-4 PowerPoint, business cards, letterhead
Integration Weeks 5-6 Pitch deck, proposal templates, training
Governance Ongoing Quarterly audits, regulatory updates

  • Ensuring regulatory compliance

Board Review
